Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

Musketeer respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension does and does not collect.

1. Data Collection

Musketeer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The extension does not collect browsing history, website content, keystrokes, or personal identifiers.

2. Local Storage

Musketeer stores layout preferences (such as window positions and split ratios) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

3. Payments

Pro purchases are processed securely by ExtensionPay. Musketeer does not receive or store credit card information. ExtensionPay may collect information necessary to process payments according to their own privacy policy.

4. Permissions

Musketeer requests Chrome permissions (tabs, windows, storage, and system.display) solely to enable window splitting functionality. These permissions are not used for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

5. Third-Party Services

Musketeer does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising services.

6. Changes

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
